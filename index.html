What is our project

Our innovative graduation project at King Abdullah University of Science and Technology (KAUST) represents a breakthrough in 3D reconstruction and computer vision. We've developed a comprehensive pipeline that combines 3D Gaussian Splatting with advanced segmentation and depth estimation techniques to transform 2D images into detailed 3D models with remarkable accuracy.

Advanced Pipeline

Combines SAM segmentation, Depth-Anything-V2, and 3D Gaussian Splatting

Fast Processing

Efficient 3D Gaussian Splatting with accelerated rendering

High Accuracy

Precise depth estimation using Depth-Anything-V2 and COLMAP

Research Innovation

Cutting-edge research combining multiple state-of-the-art technologies
